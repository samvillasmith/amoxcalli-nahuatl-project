## Review Queue Interpretation

The review queue is intentionally broad. A row may appear because it is missing `msn_headword`, is `Classical_citation`, is `Comparative_only`, has an editorial flag, has low source confidence, or remains unreviewed.

Priority meanings:

- `P1` - blocking issue before any public MSN use, usually missing headword or editorial flag.
- `P2` - review required before promotion, usually classical, comparative, or low-confidence evidence.
- `P3` - candidate has a usable form but still lacks MSN approval.
- `P4` - ready candidate; not expected until decisions are recorded in a later phase.

This phase does not approve entries. It makes the decision surface visible.

## Authoring Pack Interpretation

The authoring pack is a drafting aid, not a publication approval. Its `candidate_internal_use` rows are source-backed EHN entries suitable for controlled internal writing experiments. Public release still requires MSN approval, register QA, and source notes.

## D19 Children Book Interpretation

`d19_children_book/` is a D19 controlled drafting packet for **A Day In The Milpa**. It includes controlled MSN local draft evidence, 59 selected vocabulary rows, 7 sentence patterns, 22 glossary rows, 7 grammar decisions, 8 avoided/deferred term records, release-planning files, and a QA report.

Current QA status: 6 PASS, 1 WARN, 1 BLOCKER. The packet is usable as internal drafting evidence, but public release is blocked until whole-MSN validation approves the relevant grammar, vocabulary, register boundaries, glossary, and publication safeguards.

## Capstone Learner Book Interpretation

`learn_modern_standard_nahuatl_plan.md` records the intended final book: **Learn Modern Standard Nahuatl**. This is not an early demonstration text. It is the end-of-process synthesis that should be written after the grammar, lexicon, examples, validation process, and public release conventions are stable.
